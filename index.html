Welcome To The WexEnergy Dream Team Senior Project

Sponsoring Organization: WexEnergy

Project Synopsis:


WexEnergy is a company which sells WexWindows which are installed onto windows. They reduce heating and cooling costs for buildings as well as their overall carbon footprint.
It is our task to create a workflow app that will streamline the way their employees and other businesses capture the data needed for window installation and manufacturing.
This App will be able to be launched on any web and phone platform as we're utilizing the flutter framework.
