Project Synopsis:

WexEnergy is a company which installs windows in such a way that reduces heating costs for buildings which also reduces their carbon footprint. It is our task to create a workflow app that will streamline the way their employees and other businesses capture the data needed for window installation such as Length x Width and more. This App will be able to be launched on any web and phone platform as we're utilizing the flutter framework.
